About me

Software Developer | Electrical Engineering Technologist | Tech Enthusiast

Ever since I was a kid I've always thought technology and the things it can do for us was so amazing. Even now there are trillions of electrons coursing through whatever device you are reading this on, smashing into each other in unbridled chaos that we orchestrate into a careful harmony so we can watch cat videos and chat with our friends... neat!

It was that sense of amazement that lead to me spending 8 years training and working as an Electrical Engineering Technologist in various roles across Canada. I have seen projects through from initial design to quality testing and have the skills to contribute at every step of the process between. As I moved from fabrication work building and helping to design control systems, to designing and prototyping new products, and eventually onto comissioning and maintenance of industrial machinery, I picked up tons of hands-on skills and experience that always left me hungry to learn more.

Its still this sense of amazement that drives me to continue learning every day and what ultimately prompted me to start learning Software Development. I figured since I can comfortably design and build a circuit full of LEDs, sensors, motors and other components, I may as well learn how to write the code to make all the lights flash in pretty patterns too!
